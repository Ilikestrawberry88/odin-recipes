Pizza recipe.

Description.

Are you ready to make a delicious homemade pizza? Follow these easy steps to create a mouth-watering pizza that’s sure to please!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water.
  • 1 packet active dry yeast.
  • 3 3/4 cups bread flour.
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil.
  • 2 teaspoons salt.
  • 1 teaspoon sugar.
  • Pizza sauce.
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded.
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives).

Instructions:

  1. Proof the yeast: In a large bowl, combine the warm water and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes, until the yeast is dissolved and foamy
  2. Make the dough: Add the flour, olive oil, salt, and sugar to the bowl. Mix until a shaggy dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough: Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for 7-10 minutes, until the dough is smooth and elastic.
  4. Let the dough rise: Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours, until it has doubled in size.
  5. Preheat the oven: Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  6. Shape the dough: Punch down the dough and shape it into a circle or rectangle, depending on your preference.
  7. Top the dough: Spread the pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Add your desired toppings.
  8. Bake the pizza: Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Serve: Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Slice and serve hot!

Nutrition Information:

  • Calories: 322
  • Fat: 10g
